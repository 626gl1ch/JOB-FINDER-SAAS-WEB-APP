User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

SnipeJob ("we", "us") values your data autonomy. According to Facebook/Meta Developer Platform policies, Google Cloud OAuth Platform guidelines, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and applicable privacy laws, users have the right to request full erasure of personal information collected by SnipeJob.

Option 1: Meta / Facebook App Deauthorization & Automatic Deletion

If you created your SnipeJob account using Facebook or Instagram login and wish to remove SnipeJob's access and delete stored data, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook Account.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  3. In the left sidebar, select Apps and Websites.
  4. Search for SnipeJob in the list of active apps.
  5. Click Remove next to SnipeJob.
  6. Select the option to remove all stored data, posts, and permissions. Facebook will trigger our automated deletion webhook, completely unlinking your OAuth identifier and marking your account profile data for purge.

Option 2: Google & LinkedIn Account Revocation

If you registered using Google or LinkedIn OAuth:

  1. Go to your Google Security Settings (Third-party apps) or LinkedIn Permitted Services.
  2. Locate SnipeJob and click Remove Access / Revoke.

Option 3: Manual Direct Data Purge Request

You can request complete account and data removal directly from our engineering team at any time:

  1. Send an email to support@snipejob.app from the email address associated with your SnipeJob account.
  2. Use the Subject line: Account & Data Deletion Request.
  3. In the body of the email, state: "I hereby request the permanent deletion of my account and all personal data associated with SnipeJob."

Our team will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation code confirming complete permanent removal from our primary database servers.

What Information is Deleted?

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data is permanently purged from our active systems:

  • Your user profile, email address, full name, and avatar URL.
  • All uploaded resume documents, parsed skill profiles, and tailored proposal drafts.
  • All pinned jobs, custom alerts, and sector filtering preferences.
  • All third-party social authentication tokens (Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, Instagram).

Note: Anonymized transaction invoices (required by law for tax reporting) will be retained for statutory record-keeping periods without linking back to your deleted personal identity.
