Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Thank you for using SnipeJob. We strive to offer the most responsive job aggregation and AI application assistance suite for freelancers.

Digital Service Billing Policy

Because SnipeJob provides immediate digital access to real-time scraped job streams, instant AI proposal parsing (via Gemini API), and premium sector unlock privileges upon payment processing, all subscription charges (both monthly and annual plans) processed via Paystack or NOWPayments are non-refundable.

1. Managing & Canceling Subscriptions

You can cancel your active subscription at any time directly through your Account Settings within the platform. Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the paid billing cycle.
  • You will not be billed for any subsequent cycles.
  • No pro-rated refunds or credits will be issued for partial subscription periods.

2. Payment Gateway Specifics

  • Paystack Payments (Card/Bank): Card transactions processed through Paystack are subject to Paystack's transaction processing policies. Duplicate charges caused by gateway errors will be audited and refunded to the original payment method upon verification.
  • Cryptocurrency Payments (NOWPayments): Due to the irreversible nature of blockchain transactions, cryptocurrency payments are strictly non-refundable once confirmed on the network.

3. Limited Special Exceptions

Refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis under the following verified conditions:

  • Verified Platform Downtime: If a server-side error prevents your access to the Service for more than five (5) consecutive business days, and our support team is unable to restore access.
  • Proven Gateway Overcharge: In the event of confirmed duplicate billing resulting from payment processor transmission errors.

4. How to Submit a Billing Inquiry

If you experience a technical billing error, please contact billing support at support@snipejob.app within seven (7) days of the transaction. Please include your registered email address, transaction reference/invoice ID, and proof of payment.
