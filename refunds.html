Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing SnipeJob. We aim to provide the best career acceleration tools for freelancers.

No-Refunds Policy

Due to the digital nature of our Service — including instant access to premium scraped job feeds, immediate generation of AI-tailored proposals, ATS resume scoring, and KYC validation — all subscription charges (including monthly and annual plans) are non-refundable.

1. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time. To avoid future billing, you must cancel your subscription before the next renewal date. You can cancel your subscription inside the app via Settings → Current Plan → Manage Subscription (which redirects to the Stripe Billing Portal).

Upon cancellation, you will retain full access to all Pro features until the end of your current active billing period. Once the period ends, your account will automatically downgrade to the Free tier, and you will not be charged again.

2. Exceptions & Billing Errors

We review refund requests on a case-by-case basis under the following limited circumstances:

  • Technical Malfunction: If a persistent system error completely prevents you from accessing the Service for a prolonged period, and our support team cannot resolve it within five (5) business days of being notified.
  • Duplicate Billing: If our payment processor mistakenly charges you twice for the same subscription period, we will issue a refund for the duplicate transaction.
  • Legal Mandate: In jurisdictions where local consumer protection laws mandate a cooling-off period for digital subscriptions, we will honor those refund requests in accordance with the law.

3. Side Task Rewards (Withdrawals)

Withdrawals requested from your wallet balance are subject to validation checks (including fraud and VPN audits). If a withdrawal request is denied due to a violation of our Terms of Service (e.g. task fraud or location masking), the associated earnings are permanently forfeited and will not be refunded or paid out.

4. How to Request Support

If you believe you qualify for a refund under section 2, please email us at support@snipejob.app with the following information:

  • Your account email address;
  • Stripe checkout session ID or invoice ID;
  • A detailed explanation of the issue or billing error; and
  • Any screenshots showing errors or malfunction.

We will respond to all support requests within three (3) business days.
