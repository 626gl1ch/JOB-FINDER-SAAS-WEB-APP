Our average is under 30 seconds from when a job is posted to when you receive the notification. Pro users are on our priority queue — usually under 10 seconds. Free users are on the standard queue — typically 15–45 seconds.
Side Task is a section in your dashboard where you'll find short tasks — surveys, app trials, sign-up offers — matched specifically to your country. For Side Task earnings, yes — you'll need to verify your phone number and a valid government ID. This is to prevent fraud and ensure tasks are matched to the right country. It only needs to be done once and takes about 2 minutes. Your ID data is encrypted and never shared.
