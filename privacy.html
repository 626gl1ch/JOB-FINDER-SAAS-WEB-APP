Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

At SnipeJob, accessible from SnipeJob website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SnipeJob and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@snipejob.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Account Details: When you register for an account, we collect your full name, email address, country, and exp_level. If you use OAuth providers (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram), we receive authentication identifiers and basic profile details from those networks.
  • Professional Profile & Resumes: To power the AI tailoring and interview prep tools, we collect the text, skills, education, bio, and file content of any resume you upload or paste.
  • KYC Documents: If you verify your identity, we process your phone number, edge IP-based country code, and images of government-issued IDs. These images are scanned for fraud detection and deleted immediately after processing.
  • Network and Usage Logs: We collect your IP address, browser type, device details, and geographic location to identify potential fraud (such as VPN usage during Side Tasks).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various operational purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and personalize our job alerts and resume optimization tools;
  • To manage your Pro subscription and process Stripe payments;
  • To verify completions of monetization offers (Side Tasks) and prevent fraud;
  • To send you transactional emails, renewal notices, and subscription expiry alerts;
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues.

3. Third-Party Integrations & Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party partners to facilitate key platform features. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Supabase: Serves as our primary database hosting and user authentication system.
  • Stripe: Processes subscription checkout sessions and portal updates. We do not store or directly handle your credit card details.
  • Google Gemini API: Powers our AI resume parsing, proposal writing, ATS scoring, and interview question scoring.
  • Resend: Operates our email delivery engine (e.g. sending warning alerts and notifications).
  • CPALead / OfferToro: Delivers monetized CPA offers. We share an encrypted or randomized user ID (subid) with these networks to match completions back to your balance.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use standard session cookies and local storage tokens to keep you logged in and preserve your workspace settings. These technologies do not track your browsing habits across other websites.

Most web browsers allow you to disable cookies in settings, but doing so will prevent you from logging in and utilizing our core dashboard features.

5. Data Retention

We retain your profile data, pinned jobs, and resume text for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will purge all associated data from our active database systems within 30 days, except where retention is legally required to resolve disputes, comply with tax laws, or prevent recurring fraud.

6. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. We utilize encrypted HTTPS connections, Row-Level Security (RLS) on database levels, and strict environment isolation to protect your information, but cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may possess specific rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your data. You can perform most of these updates directly inside your Settings dashboard, or request full account deletion by contacting support@snipejob.app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
