Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

At SnipeJob ("we", "us", "our"), accessible at https://626gl1ch.github.io/JOB-FINDER-SAAS-WEB-APP/, protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and data safety of our users ("you", "your") is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how personal data is collected, processed, stored, shared, and deleted when you interact with our platform, API, and associated services.

If you have questions or require further information regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at support@snipejob.app.

1. Information We Collect

We process personal data required to deliver real-time job alerts, AI resume proposal tailoring, subscription billing, and fraud prevention:

  • Account & Authentication Identifiers: When you register directly or sign up via social single sign-on (OAuth providers including Google, Meta/Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram), we collect your email address, full name, profile picture URL, and third-party authentication tokens.
  • Professional Profile & Resumes: To power AI-driven proposal writing and ATS scoring, we collect your primary skills, experience level, targeted sectors, bio, pasted resume content, and uploaded resume document files.
  • Financial & Payment Records: Payment transactions are handled securely through accredited gateways (Paystack for card/bank transfers and NOWPayments for cryptocurrency transactions). We process payment session tokens, subscription tier statuses, and billing references. We do not store full credit card numbers or raw wallet private keys on our servers.
  • Network, Device & Anti-Fraud Logs: We log IP addresses, user-agent strings, cloud network routing details, and edge geographic data to enforce regional compliance, prevent abusive multi-accounting, and detect proxy/VPN location spoofing during task completions.

2. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information under the following lawful bases:

  • Performance of Contract: To deliver instant freelance job alerts, generate tailored proposal templates, unlock sector feeds, and manage subscription billing.
  • Consent: When you grant single-sign-on (OAuth) permissions via Google, Meta/Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram, or voluntarily upload your resume for parsing.
  • Legitimate Interests: To detect security breaches, enforce anti-fraud policies on side-task offer walls, protect against bot automation, and resolve user technical issues.

3. Third-Party Service Providers & Data Processing

We share data only with infrastructure partners who comply with strict data security standards (GDPR, CCPA, ISO27001):

  • Supabase Inc.: Cloud database storage, Row-Level Security (RLS) enforcement, and user authentication infrastructure.
  • Cloudflare Inc.: Serverless Worker processing at edge locations, DDoS mitigation, and firewall security.
  • Google Gemini AI (Google LLC): Natural language processing model used strictly for parsing resume text and formatting proposal suggestions. Data transmitted to Gemini is ephemeral and not retained for public model training.
  • Paystack & NOWPayments: Secure payment processing gateways handling subscription billing and cryptocurrency invoices.
  • Resend Technologies: Transactional email service for delivering subscription status and security alerts.
  • CPALead / OfferToro: Monetization offer providers. We transmit randomized sub-IDs (non-personally identifiable strings) to verify task completions.

4. Cookies and Web Storage

We store encrypted local storage tokens and essential session state directly in your browser to maintain your active login and user preferences. We do not place cross-site tracking advertising cookies or sell user data to third-party ad networks.

5. Data Deletion Instructions & User Rights

In accordance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA) and Meta/Google/LinkedIn Developer Guidelines, you hold full control over your personal data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can purge your profile data, pinned jobs, and stored resumes at any time through your Account Settings inside the application.
  • Social Login Revocation: You may revoke SnipeJob's permissions at any time via your Facebook Settings (Apps & Websites), Google Account Security, or LinkedIn Authorized Apps.
  • Direct Deletion Requests: You can submit a formal data deletion request by visiting our dedicated Data Deletion Instructions Page or emailing support@snipejob.app with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Upon request, all profile records, resumes, and saved data will be permanently deleted from our active databases within 30 days.

6. Data Security Measures

We employ enterprise-grade security controls including end-to-end HTTPS/TLS encryption, automated database Row-Level Security (RLS) policies, and strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your personal data.

7. Compliance & Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by court order, law enforcement request, or applicable law to protect SnipeJob's legal rights, prevent physical harm, or investigate financial fraud.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy periodically. Revisions will be published on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our Service constitutes acceptance of the modified Privacy Policy.
